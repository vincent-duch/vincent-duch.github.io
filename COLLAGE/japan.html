DIVERCITY TOKYO PLAZA, JAPAN

DiverCity Tokyo Plaza opened in 2021 and is located in Odaiba, Japan where it shows the shopping mall designed with the theme of theatrical city space. The complex has seven floors consisting of stores of American apparel stores, and Japanese souvenir shops. The Gundam Base Tokyo which is also located in the DiverCity Plaza showcases the 1/1 scale Gundam Unicorn which is from the store dedicated to Gundam, a Japanese Mecha animated series, which serves as the museum and store for fans of Gundam and also to the tourist.



HOW TO GET THERE

-Ride the train from Doroteo Jose LRT 1 up to EDSA station.

-Upon arriving at the EDSA station, you need to ride a bus to take you to the NAIA Terminal 1.

-Take the flight schedule from Manila to Tokyo Narita.

-Upon arrival at the Narita Airport Terminal 2-3 Station, You need to ride a train going from Narita to Odaiba-Kaihinkoen.
