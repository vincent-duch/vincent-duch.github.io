ELIZABETH TOWER, ENGLAND

Elizabeth Tower also known as Big Ben Tower, is a clock tower located in Westminster, London, the capital of England, it is widely known for its time accuracy and its massive hour bell. The clock was designed by Edmund Beckett Denison with Sir George Airy and the clockmaker Edward Dent while the tower was designed by Augustus Pugin in a neo-Gothic Style. It was completed in May 31, 1859 although Edward Dent was not able to see the completion of the project it was finished by his son, Frederick Dent.
