THE COLOSSEUM, ITALY

The Colosseum is an oval amphitheater located in the city of Rome, Italy. It is considered as the largest ancient amphitheater ever built and despite its age the Colosseum still stands up to the current timeline. The Colosseum was created during 70-80 AD built under the emperor Vespasian and was completed in AD 8- under his successor, Titus, it was designed to host gladiatorial shows as well as variety of other events. In the current timeline, the Colosseum serves as a major tourist attraction in Rome which shows the parts several subterranean passageways used to transport wild animals and gladiator to the arena.
