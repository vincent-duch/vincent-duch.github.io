HACKSAW RIDGE (2016)


Genre: Action, War film

Hacksaw Ridge (2016), focuses on the World War II War Veteran Desmond Doss, an American pacifist combat medic in which he refuses to carry or use any kinds of firearm due to Doss's religious beliefs. The film depicts Doss's heroic act when he risked his life by saving 75 men all by himself in the Battle of Okinawa by carrying the wounded bodies of his fellow soldiers to the cliff's edge and belaying them down by rope without firing any kinds of weapon. Doss was awarded the Medal of Honor by President Truman due to his heroic act of saving 75 soldiers at the Hacksaw Ridge.

Sixty years later, Frodo was able to acquire the One Ring due to his uncle which is the main trigger of the story which is the quest of Frodo and his eight companions to their journey to Mount Doom in the land of Mordor which is the only palce where the One Ring can be destroyed. There are 2 sequels of the film which is the The Two Towers (2002) and The Return of the King (2003),which serves as the final story of the Fellowship of the Ring.
